Industrial pollution is when factories,mines and transportion release harmful substances into the air. These pollutants can cause health problems such as respiratory disease ,cancer ,astma and decreased lung function.

The Arctic is warming about twice as fast as the global average , causing the ice that polar bears depend on to melt away . Loss of sea ice also threatens the bears main prey, seals, which need the ice to taise their young.

Wate pollution is the release of substances into bodies of water that makes water unsafe for human use and disrupts aquatic ecosystems.

Deforestation is the purposeful clearing of forested land . Throught history and into modern times ,forests have been razed to make space for agriculture and animal frazing and to obtain wood for fuel ,manufacturing and construction.
